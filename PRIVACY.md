# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: 2026-03-02*

Dauphin ("the App") is an open-source, unofficial companion app for Tamkang University students, developed by Tamkang University Information Technology and Open Culture Community and other contributors ("we", "us"). This policy describes how the App handles your information.

## Data We Collect

### Student Identification Number

When you log in through Tamkang University's SSO system, the App derives a TKU iLife token from the authentication process. Your student identification number is used to fetch your course schedule and generate your student ID barcode.

### Course Schedule

Your course data is fetched from university services and cached locally on your device for offline access.

## How Data Is Stored

- Your student identification number is stored in the device Keychain (encrypted by iOS).
- Course data and login state are stored in local App Group storage on your device.
- **All data stays on your device.** We do not operate any servers and do not collect, transmit, or store your data externally.

## Third-Party Services

The App communicates directly with Tamkang University's official services to authenticate and retrieve course data. We do not control those services; refer to Tamkang University's privacy policy for how they handle your information.

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal data to any third party.

## Data Deletion

You can remove all stored data at any time by logging out within the App or by deleting the App from your device. Logging out clears your student identification number, cached courses, and all web session data.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App's GitHub repository at https://github.com/tkuitocc/dauphin.
